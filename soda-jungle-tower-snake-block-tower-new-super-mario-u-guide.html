Star Coins

advertisement

Star Coin 1 - Not hard to miss when riding the giant snake block. Just stay at the front, grab it, and jump over the electrified enemy.

Star Coin 2 - After the red circle you'll find the smaller snake block out in front, moving to the left. Stay with it and grab the second Star Coin. Stay moving before it doubles back and traps you.

Star Coin 3 - In the second area with the snake blocks, the large and small snake blocks will diverge, stay with the smaller snake blocks to grab the Star Coin, then immediately jump back to the large snake blocks.
